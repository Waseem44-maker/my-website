HEC Need-Based Scholarships

Undergraduate & Graduate Programs

All HEC Recognized Universities Full Tuition + Stipend Deadline: 15 Nov 2023

The Higher Education Commission offers need-based scholarships for talented students who cannot afford higher education. Covers tuition fees and provides monthly stipends for undergraduate and graduate programs.

Eligibility:
  • Minimum 70% marks in last degree
  • Family income less than Rs. 45,000/month
  • Admission in HEC recognized university
  • No other scholarship availed

Top Pakistani Universities Full Tuition + Expenses Deadline: 30 Oct 2023

The Balochistan Education Endowment Fund (BEEF) offers merit scholarships for outstanding students from Balochistan pursuing higher education in engineering, medicine, and technology fields.

Eligibility:

For Pakistani Students

HEC Overseas Scholarships

PhD Programs Abroad

Top World Universities Full Funding Deadline: 15 Jan 2024

HEC offers fully funded PhD scholarships for Pakistani students at top universities worldwide. Covers tuition, living allowance, airfare, and health insurance for duration of PhD program.

Eligibility:
  • 18 years of education with minimum 3.0 CGPA
  • GRE/GMAT as per university requirements
  • IELTS 6.5 or TOEFL 88
  • Maximum age 40 years
